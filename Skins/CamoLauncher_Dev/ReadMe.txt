CamoLauncher by Aryan Tyagi
___________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________

False windows-like icon that is actually your app launcher. No one will ever doubt that this is not a regular desktop icon.

HOW TO ADD APPS?
-->right click > custom skin actions > addapps
-->a folder will popup.
-->put shortcuts of the applicantions you want to open
-->Ta-da

HOW TO MATCH FALSE ICON WITH STANDARD WINDOW ICON?(win7) 
-->right click > custom skin actions > change variables
-->change the first six variables to match your settings
-->ta-da
